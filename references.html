About This Research

Extended Project Qualification

This website presents the findings of an EPQ research project examining the structural, professional, and political factors that contribute to the marginalization of Irish history in UK education.

The research employs a multi-method approach including curriculum document analysis, academic literature review, textbook studies, and comparative analysis with Northern Ireland's educational framework.

This research was conducted as part of an Extended Project Qualification (EPQ). The EPQ is an independent research project taken alongside A-levels, requiring students to plan, research, and produce a substantial piece of work on a topic of their choosing.
